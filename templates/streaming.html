Stream Overlay Generator

This tool allows you to create overlays for your transcribing streams. They can easily be integrated into your OBS Studio setup.

OBS Studio Instructions

  1. Configure your overlay and copy the link
  2. Open OBS Studio and add a new Browser Source
  3. Paste the link in the URL field
  4. The width and height are not really important, but I recommend to reduce them to something like 300x100
  5. Position the overlay in the corner that you configured
